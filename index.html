    Computer Machine Procurement

    At Neotech Energy Services, we provide expert assistance in sourcing the right computing solutions tailored to your business needs. Whether you're setting up a new office, upgrading existing systems, or enhancing your network infrastructure, we help you select and procure machines that ensure optimal performance and productivity.

    Key Features:

    • Tailored Selection: We evaluate your business requirements to recommend machines with the best specifications for your tasks—whether for office work, design, programming, or data analysis.
    • Comprehensive Specifications: We ensure the systems have the right balance of CPU power, RAM, storage capacity, graphics processing capabilities, and operating system compatibility for seamless performance.
    • Reliable Supplier Partnerships: We collaborate with trusted vendors to provide high-quality machines with reliable warranty and support options.
    • Budget-Conscious Solutions: Our team works within your budget while ensuring you get the most cost-effective yet high-performance machines.
    • Scalability & Future-Proofing: We help you choose machines that can be easily upgraded or expanded as your needs grow, saving you from costly future overhauls.
    • Energy Efficiency: We prioritize energy-efficient systems that reduce running costs and support your sustainability goals.

    Procurement Process:

    1. Needs Assessment: We discuss your needs, analyze tasks, and propose the best machine configurations.
    2. Vendor Research & Selection: After thorough research, we suggest the most reputable suppliers, comparing costs and warranty terms.
    3. Procurement: Once approved, we handle the purchasing process, ensuring timely delivery and installation.
    4. Installation & Testing: We set up the machines, ensuring that they meet all requirements and work seamlessly.
    5. Post-Sale Support: Our team provides ongoing support for troubleshooting, upgrades, and maintenance.

    Computer Repairs

    Neotech Services offers comprehensive computer repair services to ensure your devices run smoothly, minimizing downtime and increasing productivity. We specialize in diagnosing and repairing both hardware and software issues to keep your systems operating at their best.

    Key Features:

    • Hardware Repairs: From fixing slow performance due to faulty hard drives to repairing damaged motherboards, we handle all hardware issues with precision and expertise.
    • Software Troubleshooting: Whether it’s a malfunctioning OS, application crashes, or security issues, we fix software-related problems to get your system back on track.
    • Upgrades & Replacements: We recommend hardware upgrades to improve performance and extend the life of your machines. Our repairs often include replacing outdated or faulty components such as RAM, hard drives, or power supplies.
    • Preventative Maintenance: Regular maintenance ensures your machines perform at optimal levels. We clean dust, replace worn-out parts, and ensure your system’s cooling mechanisms are working properly.
    • Data Recovery: If your hard drive crashes, we offer data recovery services to help retrieve vital information from damaged or corrupted storage.

    Repair Process:

    1. Diagnosis: We conduct a thorough inspection of your device, identifying the root cause of the issue.
    2. Cost Estimation & Approval: After diagnosing the issue, we provide a clear cost estimate and get your approval before proceeding.
    3. Repair & Replacement: Our expert technicians carry out the necessary repairs or component replacements.
    4. Post-Repair Testing: Once the repair is complete, we test the system to ensure everything works properly.
    5. Support & Guidance: We provide you with useful tips for maintaining your system, along with post-repair support to resolve any follow-up issues.
